Spain is a country located in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula to the south of France and Andorra and to the east of Portugal. It has coastlines on the Bay of Biscay (a part of the Atlantic Ocean) and the Mediterranean Sea. Spain's capital and largest city is Madrid, and the country is known for its long history, unique culture, strong economy, and very high living standards. Spain is also known for its food, Flamenco music and dance, siesta, bullfights, horses, arts and literature, architecture, Moorish heritage, its islands, Mediterranean beaches, wines, fruits and vegetables, and football.
